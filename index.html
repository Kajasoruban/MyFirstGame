instructions


You are the blue monster and periodically eggs will be spawn around you. also enemies will be apear. Once egg spawned it will hatch into larva after 10 s . Eggs can be move away by enemies but if it's in larva form it will be eaten by enemy. So you have to protect eggs and larva from enemy by move eggs and larva to bushes in top to get scores. you have to score 30 to finish game but if you lost 5 larva you will be lost even if you scored 30. You can move the monster by drag it with curser or click anywhere in game.


*click 'd' while playing to apply debug mode*
